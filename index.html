About Us

This is a Hip Hop Night designed to have the enthusiasm of a concert with the opportunity for networking and discovering great Christian Hip Hop artists. Live DJs, Hip Hop Artists from the Southeast Region of the USA and beyond, great people and its Rated E for Everyone. No Cover Charge (contributions will be accepted). This Underground Christian Hip Hop Experience is one you will never forget. Great for youth groups, a night out for adults or anyone who is looking for good clean fun in Georgia. We are not an open mic event and all of our artists are booked in advance. If you would like to submit your music for consideration, please email us at The47SpotEmail@email.com
